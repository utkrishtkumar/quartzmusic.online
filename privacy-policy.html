Privacy Policy

Effective April 16, 2026

Quartz Music Player is built with a strict privacy-first philosophy. We do not collect, store, transmit, or process any personal data or user information. Your music library, listening habits, and device information remain entirely on your device.

Information We Do Not Collect

To be unambiguous, we do not collect personal identifiers, device identifiers, location data, analytics, listening history, crash reports, advertising identifiers, cookies, tracking pixels, or cross-app tracking data.

Permissions and Their Purpose

Quartz Music Player requests only the minimum permissions necessary for audio playback, USB DAC support, Bluetooth routing, notifications, local media access, and premium license verification through Google Play.

Third-Party Services

Quartz Music Player uses Google Play Billing for premium purchases and Google Play Integrity for installation authenticity. We do not receive payment information and do not integrate advertising networks, analytics platforms, social SDKs, or data brokers.

Data Storage

All application data, including your music library index, equalizer settings, and playback preferences, is stored locally on your device. Nothing is transmitted to external servers.

Children's Privacy

Quartz Music Player does not collect data from any user, including children.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the revised version will be published at this URL.
